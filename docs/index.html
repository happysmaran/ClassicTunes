ClassicTunes

Bringing back the classic iTunes experience

About

ClassicTunes is a modern macOS app built from scratch using Swift and SwiftUI, faithfully recreating the look and feel of iTunes 7–10 on Apple Silicon Macs.

  • Working Cover Flow
  • Playlists
  • MiniPlayer
  • Classic iTunes 7–10 aesthetics

System requirements: macOS 26 Tahoe or later, Apple Silicon (M1 and later).
